All about me!

I fell in love with general health and fitness when I was 15. I knew from the day I started I originally wanted to lose weight like any 15 year old girl. This was the era of “thigh gaps” and “skinny teas” in 2012. I remember looking in the mirror that I would do anything to be thin.

At 15 years old it started out as something I need to do for my health because I was definitely over weight and needed to lose weight for my hockey game. The first 20 pounds came off easily as I became more active and ate healthy foods.

It quickly became an obsession and a case of orthorexia.

I was:

  • Cutting out “bad foods” from my diet
  • Avoiding social situations and showing high levels of stress where the food was unhealthy
  • Obsessively concerned about weight gain and food on myfitnesspal

Unfortunately this continued for years with big periods of under eating and binging at night. This was until 2017 I came to terms with my increase of muscle mass and weight gain from Powerlifting. I found IIFYM. IIFYM (If It Fits Your Macros) saved my life because it allowed me to still eat foods on my “bad” foods list and be flexible eating out. I realized that no food is “bad” and that more food in general could GREATLY help my performance. I may now weigh more than I have never looked better because my muscles are FULL, I am able to PUSH hard in the gym, and my macros are BALANCED, as well as my life.

And now, with my B.S. in nutrition and Personal Training certification, I want to help people escape those disordered thoughts and become their strongest selves by doing the same.
